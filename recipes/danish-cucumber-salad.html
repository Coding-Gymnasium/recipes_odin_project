Danish Cucumber Salad - Agurkesalat

Description

This Scandinavian salad is a must for cucumber fans. Made from sliced cucumbers and thinly sliced onions, this is a zesty pickled salad that hits you right in the tastebuds.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers x 3
  • Salt, to taste
  • Water x 1 cup
  • White Vinegar or Lemon juice x 1 cup
  • Sugar x 4 tbsp
  • Black Pepper x 1/2 tsp
  • Onion, to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and peel cucumbers.
  2. Cut cucumbers into very thin slices and place in medium bowl.
  3. Salt lightly
  4. Let stand for 15 minutes.
  5. Pour off all liquid from cucumbers.
  6. In a small bowl mix together the water, vinegar, sugar and pepper.
  7. Thinly sliced vidalia or walla walla onions can be placed in the salad also according to taste and this is optional.
  8. Pour mixture over the cucumbers and place in the refrigerator for one hour or until chilled.
  9. Drain off liquid again or serve with a slotted spoon.
